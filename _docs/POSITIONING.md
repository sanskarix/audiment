# Audiment Positioning Strategy

## Core Positioning
Audiment is a High-Trust Accountability Engine – not generic compliance software.

## The Real Problem We Solve
Pencil-whipping: field workers faking audits, filling checklists without visiting locations, sending WhatsApp photos from yesterday. Operations heads have no ground-truth visibility into what is actually happening at their branches.

## Primary ICPs
1. VP of Operations / Director of Operations at multi-unit QSR chains
2. Franchise Operations Head at franchise networks (20-500 locations)
3. Head of Retail Operations at retail chains

## Key Differentiators (Use These in All Copy)
- Flash Verification: geo-tagged video + selfie proves auditor was physically present
- 48-hour auto-CAPA: critical failures auto-generate corrective tasks with mandatory photo proof
- Mandatory photo evidence: enforced per question – no photo, no submission
- Surprise audits: managers never know when one is coming
- Trend detection: 3 consecutive poor audits trigger a priority alert

## Competitors
- SafetyCulture (iAuditor) – generic, not India-focused, no flash verification
- GoAudits – basic checklist tool, no CAPA automation
- Zenput – US-focused, expensive, no geo-verification
- Jolt – restaurant-specific but no evidence enforcement

## Copy Tone Rules
- Punchy and outcome-driven – "Stop pencil-whipping" not "Improve compliance"
- Relatable to franchise owners – reference real pain like "the WhatsApp black hole"
- Never robotic SEO text in visible UI
- Sentence case on all headings – never Title Case
- No generic compliance-speak

## GEO Strategy
- Invisible SEO: sleek UI for humans, structured data for AI crawlers
- Semantic HTML tables for all comparison matrices
- JSON-LD schema injected per page via Next.js Script – never global
- llms.txt entity definition at public/llms.txt
- AI crawlers explicitly allowed in robots.txt
