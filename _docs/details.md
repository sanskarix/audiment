# Audiment: Product Documentation & Overview

Audiment is a next-generation, web-based audit management platform designed for multi-outlet businesses. It digitizes the entire audit lifecycle–from template creation to corrective action resolution–providing business owners with "Ground Truth" visibility across all locations.

---

## 1. The Core Problem
Business owners with multiple outlets (restaurants, retail, hotels) face a **Transparency Gap**. As a business scales, the owner can no longer be physically present at every branch. They rely on middle management (Managers) who may inadvertently or intentionally filter information.
- **Problem**: Physical/paper audits are easily faked, hard to track, and lack evidence.
- **Risk**: A single hygiene or operational failure at one branch can destroy a brand's reputation before the owner even knows it exists.

## 2. The Solution: Audiment
Audiment replaces "gut feel" and paperwork with a **tamper-proof digital system**. It ensures that every audit is verified with photos, videos, geo-tags, and timestamps, creating a permanent accountability trail.

---

## 3. Role-Based Architecture
Audiment is built on a distinct three-tier role system to ensure clear accountability:

### A. Admin (The Owner/Head Office)
- **Full Visibility**: Access to data from all locations.
- **Control**: Create/edit audit templates, add/deactivate users, and manage branch locations.
- **Strategic Insights**: View trend reports, comparative branch performance, and escalation alerts.

### B. Manager (Branch/Regional Level)
- **Execution**: Receive published audits from Admin and assign them to filtered auditors.
- **Resolution**: Responsible for resolving **Corrective Actions** generated by failed audits.
- **Monitoring**: Track auditor progress and scores within their assigned locations.

### C. Auditor (Field Level)
- **Mobile-First Execution**: Mobile-optimized interface for performing audits.
- **Evidence Gathering**: Required to take photos, videos, and notes for specific questions.
- **Offline Reliability**: Can complete audits in areas with low connectivity (Syncs when online).

## 4. Feature Deep-Dive: To The Core

Each feature in Audiment is engineered to solve a specific trust or operational problem in multi-outlet management.

---

### A. The Audit Workflow Engine: Scheduled & Recurring
**The Core**: Consistency as a Service.
- **The Mechanism**: Admins define a "Master Template" (e.g., FSSAI Hygiene) and set a recurrence rule (e.g., Every Monday at 9:00 AM). The system automatically instantiates a new audit document in Firestore at the scheduled time.
- **The Notification Logic**: At the moment of creation, the assigned Manager receives a push notification and email. If the Manager doesn't assign an Auditor within 2 hours, a "Scheduling Delay" alert is sent.
- **Implementation**: Uses a Cloud Function cron-job to pulse every hour and check for pending schedules.
- **Impact**: Removes the human error of "forgetting" to check a branch. It turns hygiene and safety from an "event" into a "habit."

### B. Stealth Publishing: Surprise Audits
**The Core**: Capturing the "Natural State" of the business.
- **The Mechanism**: Unlike scheduled audits, Surprise Audits are published "on-demand" by the Admin. They remain completely invisible on the Manager's dashboard until the moment the Auditor is at the door.
- **The Deadline Constraint**: Surprise audits carry a "Same-Day / 6-Hour" deadline. This prevents Managers from calling in extra staff or deep-cleaning the branch once they realize an audit is happening.
- **Impact**: Provides the Owner with a true snapshot of how the branch operates when they think no one is watching. It is the most effective tool for identifying systemic operational rot.

### C. Flashmob Audits™: The Browser Video Record
**The Core**: Raw, unedited visual ground truth.
- **The Mechanism**: Authorized auditors have a special "Flashmob" tab. Upon clicking, the browser requests camera access and starts a hard-coded **20-second recording timer**.
- **The "No-Edit" Rule**: The auditor cannot upload a video from their gallery; it must be recorded live within the Audiment interface. Once the 20 seconds are up, the video is immediately streamed to Firebase Storage and linked to a coordinate-stamped document.
- **Verification**: The system captures a "Selfie" of the auditor simultaneously to prevent "proxy auditing."
- **Impact**: 100% tamper-proof. It is impossible to "fudge" a 20-second live video of a messy kitchen or a crowded checkout line.

### D. The Corrective Action Engine: Closing the Loop
**The Core**: Converting failures into accountability.
- **The Trigger**: The moment an auditor clicks "No" on a Critical-severity question (e.g., "Is the fire exit blocked?"), the system forks a new document in the `correctiveActions` collection.
- **The Resolution Cycle**:
    1. **Alert**: Manager is notified of the "Open Critical Issue."
    2. **Action**: Manager must physically fix the issue.
    3. **Evidence**: Manager must return to the Audiment dashboard and upload an "After" photo of the fix.
    4. **Verification**: The Admin reviews the "Before" (from Auditor) and "After" (from Manager) photos side-by-side before clicking "Approve."
- **Impact**: Audits are no longer just "reports that sit in an inbox." They are active tasks that must be resolved to restore the branch's standing.

### E. The Weighted Scoring Engine: The Math of Risk
**The Core**: Prioritizing safety over aesthetics.
- **The Logic**: Audiment rejects "Flat Scoring" (where every question is worth 1 point). Instead, it uses a severity multiplier:
    - **Low Severity (x1)**: e.g., "Is the staff wearing the correct uniform?"
    - **Medium Severity (x1.5)**: e.g., "Are the floors free of debris?"
    - **Critical Severity (x5 + Auto-Fail Flag)**: e.g., "Is there evidence of pest activity?"
- **The Outcome**: A branch can get 95% of questions right, but if they fail a single "Critical" item, their status is flagged as "High Risk."
- **Impact**: Directs management's attention to the most dangerous operational risks first.

### F. Tamper-Proofing: Geo-Tag & Timestamp Locking
**The Core**: Eliminating "Living Room Auditing."
- **The Mechanism**: On every answer submission, the device's high-accuracy GPS coordinates are fetched. If the Auditor's coordinates are >50 meters from the Location's registered coordinates, the response is flagged with a "Location Mismatch" warning.
- **Timestamp Integrity**: The system uses **Server-side Timestamps** (FieldValue.serverTimestamp()). Even if an auditor changes their phone's clock to a previous time, the database records the actual time the data reached the server.
- **Impact**: Ensures the auditor was actually at the branch at the time they claimed to be.

---

## 5. Trend Analysis & Escalation Alerts
**The Core**: Moving from Reactive to Proactive management.
- **Pattern Matching**: The system looks at the last 3-5 audits of a specific location. If the "Hygiene" category score drops by >10% consecutively, it triggers a **"Declining Trend Alert."**
- **Escalation Tiers**:
    - **Tier 1 (Mild)**: Notification to Manager.
    - **Tier 2 (Moderate)**: Notification to Regional Manager.
    - **Tier 3 (Critical)**: SMS/Email alert directly to the Owner/Admin.
- **Impact**: Allows owners to intervene *before* a branch fails an external health inspection or loses a major customer.

## 6. Industrial Use Cases: Expanded Logic

### I. Restaurants & Cafés (Consistency & Compliance)
- **Use Case**: A QSR chain with 20 outlets.
- **Daily Routine**: Every morning at 8:00 AM, a "Pre-Opening Audit" is pushed. The manager has 30 minutes to confirm all equipment is on and staff is present.
- **Critical Check**: The Auditor performs a temperature check on the freezers. Any temp >4°C triggers an immediate alert to the maintenance team.

### II. Retail Chains (Brand Integrity)
- **Use Case**: A fashion brand with 50 stores.
- **Visual Audit**: The Auditor takes photos of the front window display. The Admin reviews these to ensure the nationwide "Summer Sale" branding is consistent everywhere.
- **Corrective Action**: If a mannequin is missing an accessory, a task is created for the store decorator to fix it by EOD.

---

## 7. Comprehensive Workflow Breakdown (The Life of an Audit)

1.  **Creation**: Admin defines a **Master Template** in the Web Dashboard.
2.  **Instantiation**: A **Snapshot** of that template is created and assigned to a specific **LocationID**.
3.  **Auditing**: The Auditor opens the **Auditee UI**. They are presented with one question at a time to ensure focus.
4.  **Submission**: Data is pushed to Firestore. **Cloud Functions** compute the score and check for failures.
5.  **Resolution**: Any failures live in the **Action Registry** until a Manager provides photo proof of a fix.
6.  **Archival**: The audit is stored in a permanent, searchable history for ISO/FSSAI compliance audits.

---

## 8. Summary of Technical Specs
- **Database**: Firebase Firestore (NoSQL) for real-time document sync.
- **Storage**: Firebase Storage for high-res audit photos and 20s Flashmob videos.
- **Auth**: Firebase Auth with Role-based claims.
- **Frontend**: Next.js (React) optimized for mobile-web performance (PWA ready).
- **Security**: Server-side rules ensuring Auditors can only see audits assigned to them.
