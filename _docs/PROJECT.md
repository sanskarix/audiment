# Audiment – Project Overview

## What is Audiment?
Audiment is a web-based audit management platform built for multi-outlet restaurant owners and businesses. It digitises the entire audit workflow – from publishing audits to reviewing results – through a transparent, role-based system accessible from any device.

## The Problem
Multi-outlet restaurant owners cannot physically be present at every branch. They rely on managers who may not always be fully transparent. Physical or scattered audit records are unreliable, easy to fake, and hard to track over time. Critical hygiene or operational issues go unnoticed until they become serious problems.

## The Solution
Audiment gives owners verified, timestamped, photo-backed audit data from every branch in one place. It replaces physical presence with a trusted digital system that creates accountability at every level – owner, manager, and auditor.

## Target Users
- Primary: Multi-outlet restaurant and café owners (5 to 30 outlets)
- Secondary: Retail chains, hotels, hospitals, facility management companies

## Three User Roles
- **Admin (Owner):** Full control. Creates locations, users, audit templates, publishes audits, views all reports.
- **Manager:** Assigned to specific locations. Assigns audits to auditors, monitors progress, resolves corrective actions.
- **Auditor:** Field-level user. Completes assigned audits, uploads photos, submits responses.

## Key Differentiators
- Flashmob Audit: Covert, unannounced video audit by specially authorised auditors
- Hybrid audit scoring with severity weighting
- Corrective action tracking that converts audit failures into actionable tasks
- Intelligent trend-based escalation alerts
- Geo-tagging and timestamp locking on every audit response
- Surprise audit scheduling with same-day deadlines
- Cross-branch performance comparison
- Offline sync for auditors in low connectivity areas

## Product Structure
- Public landing page for marketing
- Single login entry point with automatic role-based routing
- Mobile-optimised interface for auditors
- Web dashboard for managers and admins
- No native app in the testing phase – fully browser based
