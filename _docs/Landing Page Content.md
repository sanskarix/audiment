## [How It Works Section]

**Section Heading:**
From template to resolution in 6 steps.

**Step 1 – Create**
Admin builds an audit template in the dashboard. Set questions, assign severity levels, add photo/video requirements.

**Step 2 – Schedule or Publish**
Set it to recur (every Monday at 9 AM) or publish it as a surprise – invisible to managers until the auditor arrives.

**Step 3 – Assign**
Manager receives the audit and assigns it to an available auditor at the right location.

**Step 4 – Audit**
Auditor completes the inspection on their phone. One question at a time. Photos, videos, and notes captured in real-time. Works offline too.

**Step 5 – Score & Flag**
The system calculates a weighted score instantly. Critical failures trigger automatic alerts. No manual review needed to raise a red flag.

**Step 6 – Resolve & Archive**
Manager fixes the issue, uploads "after" photos. Admin compares before vs. after and approves. The audit is permanently stored for compliance records.

## [Role-Based Architecture Section]

**Section Heading:**
Three roles. Clear accountability.

**Role 1 – Admin (Owner / Head Office)**
- Full visibility across all locations
- Create and manage audit templates
- Add users, manage branches
- View trend reports and comparative performance
- Approve or reject corrective action resolutions

**Role 2 – Manager (Branch / Regional Level)**
- Receives published audits, assigns them to auditors
- Responsible for fixing failed items with photo proof
- Tracks auditor progress and scores for their locations

**Role 3 – Auditor (Field Level)**
- Mobile-first interface, one question at a time
- Captures photos, videos, GPS, and notes during audits
- Works offline – syncs automatically when back online

## [Features Section]

**Section Heading:**
Every feature exists to solve a trust or operations problem.

---

**Feature 1 – Scheduled & Recurring Audits**

**Heading:** Consistency on autopilot.

Set a template to repeat daily, weekly, or monthly. The system creates the audit automatically and notifies the manager. If the manager doesn't assign an auditor within 2 hours, a delay alert is sent.

No one "forgets" to check a branch. Hygiene and safety become a habit, not an event.

---

**Feature 2 – Surprise Audits (Stealth Publishing)**

**Heading:** See how branches run when no one's watching.

Publish an audit on-demand. It stays invisible on the manager's dashboard until the auditor is already at the door. A 6-hour same-day deadline prevents last-minute cleanup or calling in extra staff.

This is how you find systemic problems – not staged performances.

---

**Feature 3 – Flashmob Audits™ (Live Video Recording)**

**Heading:** 20 seconds of uneditable truth.

The auditor records a 20-second video directly inside the Audiment interface. No gallery uploads. No editing. The video is immediately uploaded with GPS coordinates. A selfie of the auditor is captured simultaneously to prevent proxy auditing.

You cannot fake a live video of a messy kitchen.

---

**Feature 4 – Corrective Action Engine**

**Heading:** Failures don't stay as reports. They become tasks.

When an auditor marks a critical item as failed, the system instantly creates a corrective action. The manager is alerted. They must fix the issue, upload an "after" photo, and wait for admin approval.

Admins see the "before" and "after" side by side. No issue is closed until it's actually fixed.

---

**Feature 5 – Weighted Scoring**

**Heading:** Not all failures are equal.

Questions are scored by severity:
- **Low (×1):** Staff wearing correct uniform
- **Medium (×1.5):** Floors free of debris
- **Critical (×5 + Auto-Fail):** Evidence of pest activity

A branch can pass 95% of questions but still get flagged as "High Risk" if they fail one critical item. This forces attention to the things that actually matter.

---

**Feature 6 – GPS & Timestamp Verification**

**Heading:** Proof they were actually there.

Every answer submission captures the auditor's GPS coordinates. If they're more than 50 meters from the registered branch location, the response is flagged with a "Location Mismatch" warning.

Timestamps are server-side – even if someone changes their phone clock, the database records the real time.

---

**Feature 7 – Trend Analysis & Escalation Alerts**

**Heading:** Catch declining branches before they fail.

The system monitors the last 3–5 audits per location. If a category score drops by more than 10% across consecutive audits, it triggers an alert.

**Escalation tiers:**
- **Tier 1:** Notification to the branch manager
- **Tier 2:** Alert to the regional manager
- **Tier 3:** SMS/email sent directly to the owner

You intervene before it becomes a health inspection failure or a viral customer complaint.

---

**Feature 8 – Offline Mode**

**Heading:** Works without internet. Syncs when connected.

Auditors in basements, rural locations, or areas with poor connectivity can complete audits fully offline. All data syncs automatically the moment the device reconnects.

No audit gets skipped because of a bad signal.

## [FAQ Section]

**Section Heading:**
Common questions.

**Q: Do my auditors need to download an app?**
No. Audiment is a web app optimized for mobile browsers. Open the link, log in, and start auditing. No app store needed.

**Q: What happens if the auditor doesn't have internet during an audit?**
They can complete the entire audit offline. All data – including photos and videos – syncs automatically once they're back online.

**Q: Can managers see surprise audits before the auditor arrives?**
No. Surprise audits are invisible on the manager's dashboard until the auditor begins the inspection. Managers cannot prepare in advance.

**Q: How is this different from using Google Forms or a spreadsheet?**
Google Forms can't verify GPS location, force live photo/video capture, auto-calculate weighted risk scores, trigger corrective actions, or escalate declining trends. Audiment is built specifically for verified, accountable audits across multiple locations.

**Q: Can I customize the audit templates?**
Yes. Admins create templates from scratch. Set your own questions, choose severity levels (Low, Medium, Critical), require photos or videos on specific questions, and set recurrence schedules.

**Q: Who can see what?**
Admins see all locations and all data. Managers see only their assigned locations. Auditors see only the audits assigned to them. Access is enforced at the server level.

**Q: Is my data secure?**
Yes. Data is stored in Firebase with server-side security rules. Role-based access is enforced at the database level, not just the UI. No one can access data they're not authorized to see.

**Q: What industries is this for?**
Any business operating multiple physical locations where consistent standards matter – restaurants, retail chains, hotels, warehouses, healthcare facilities, franchise operations, facility management companies.
